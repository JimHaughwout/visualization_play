Sankey Diagram of Most Frequent Criminal Charges Leading to NFL Arrests (2000-2014)

By Jim Haughwout • SEP 2014

This is a sankey diagram allowing exploration all NFL arrests for the four most frequent Criminal Charges by Team of Player (at time of arrest) to Legal Outcome. For example, Domestic Violence cases mostly arise from players in Denver; acquitals to this charge are greatly outnumbered by diversion programs, dropped cases or plea deals.

Mouse over any Team to see the distribution of Criminal Charges that stem from it. Do the same for an Criminal Charge to explore the source of charges by Team and distribution of Legal Outcomes. Mouse over an Legal Outcome to see which types of Criminal Charges most (and least) frequently lead to this result.


These four charges represent 60% of all NFL arrests. For more information visit my Sankey of all 51 Criminal Charges (it is much more busy). You can find a complete posting on my analysis of all NFL arrests here.
